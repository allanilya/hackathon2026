## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Slider ("we", "our", or "the Add-in") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our PowerPoint add-in.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Content Data**: Text, images, and files you choose to process through the add-in
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you use the add-in features

### Automatically Collected Data
- **Technical Data**: Browser type, PowerPoint version, and basic device information
- **Performance Data**: Error logs and performance metrics to improve the service

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Generate AI-powered PowerPoint slides based on your input
- Analyze images and suggest optimal layouts
- Process document content (DOCX, CSV files)
- Improve and optimize the add-in's functionality
- Provide customer support

## Third-Party Services

Slider integrates with the following third-party services:

### OpenAI (GPT-4)
- **Purpose**: AI-powered content generation and image analysis
- **Data Shared**: Text content and images you choose to process
- **Privacy Policy**: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

### Supabase
- **Purpose**: Data storage and user preferences
- **Data Shared**: User preferences and generated content (if saved)
- **Privacy Policy**: https://supabase.com/privacy

## Data Storage and Security

- Content is processed in real-time and not permanently stored unless you explicitly save it
- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data
- Saved drafts are stored securely and associated with your session

## Data Retention

- **Temporary Processing Data**: Deleted immediately after slide generation
- **Saved Drafts**: Retained until you delete them
- **Usage Analytics**: Aggregated and anonymized after 90 days

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt-out of analytics collection
- Export your saved drafts
- Withdraw consent at any time
